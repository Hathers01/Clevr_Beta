Our Story

CLEVR was founded in 2025 with a simple vision: to make high-quality education accessible to everyone, wherever they are. We believe that every student has unique potential, and our task is to provide the right tools and guidance to unlock it.

Our Mission

To provide an innovative, personalized, and affordable learning platform to empower the next generation to succeed academically and personally.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in virtual tutoring that drives adaptive intelligence and critical thinking.
