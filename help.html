Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I book a tutoring session?

You can book a session via the "Find a Tutor" page in the main navigation. Select the subject, time, and suitable tutor, then confirm your payment using CLOVER tokens or other options.

How do I purchase CLOVER tokens?

Navigate to the Pricing page, select the bundle that suits your needs (Single Session or Value Pack), and proceed to checkout using your preferred payment method like GoPay, OVO, or Bank Transfer.

Can I reschedule a session?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before the session starts without any penalty. Go to "My Sessions" in your dashboard to manage your bookings.
